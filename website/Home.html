The Objective

To systematically organize the archives (audio & video) of Srila Gurudeva.

Background

Over the years more than 10000 lectures of Srila Gurudeva were recorded.

Since the recording, especially in the earlier years, was not undertaken systematically, multiple challenges remain to be tackled in the archives, such as: 

  • Undated lectures 
  • Fused lectures 
  • Duplicates
  • Impaired sound quality, etc.
